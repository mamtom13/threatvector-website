RESPONSIBLE USE POLICY

Last updated: March 2024
Purpose

This policy establishes the standards of responsible use that apply to all individuals who submit abuse reports, use our lookup tools, or otherwise interact with this platform.

Permitted Use

Permitted uses include: submitting good-faith DNS abuse reports for malware C2, botnet, phishing, pharming, and spam-enabling domains; using URL Check, Hash Lookup, and WHOIS tools for legitimate abuse investigation; accessing documentation for educational purposes.

Prohibited Use

The following are strictly prohibited:
• Submitting false, misleading, or fabricated abuse reports
• Using tools to harass or investigate individuals without legitimate cause
• Submitting reports intended to cause unjustified domain suspensions (reverse abuse)
• Automated bulk querying beyond reasonable research use
• Attempting to probe or attack our infrastructure
• Submitting content disputes, copyright claims, or general complaints outside DNS abuse scope

Good-Faith Reporting

All reports must be submitted in good faith with a reasonable belief that the reported domain is engaged in DNS abuse as defined by ICANN. Reports must include verifiable technical evidence. Submitting reports with intent to harm a legitimate domain operator is misuse and may be reported to authorities.

Evidence Requirements

All reports require supporting evidence. Accepted types include: sandbox analysis reports, network packet captures (PCAP), passive DNS records, malware sample hashes, screenshots with timestamps, and URLScan.io reports. Reports without evidence will not be processed.

Reporter Accountability

By submitting a report, you represent that the information is accurate to the best of your knowledge and that you are acting in good faith. We reserve the right to decline any report that does not meet our evidence and good-faith standards.

Enforcement

Violations may result in rejection of current and future submissions and notification to relevant authorities in cases of deliberate abuse. We reserve the right to update this policy at any time.
