ABOUT

ThreatVector Intelligence is a specialist DNS abuse investigation and reporting organization focused on evidence-based identification of malicious internet infrastructure.

We operate at the intersection of DNS abuse analysis, malware infrastructure research, and responsible disclosure — bridging the gap between threat identification and effective registrar and registry mitigation.

Our methodology is built on a single principle: every report must be defensible. We investigate, validate, correlate, and document — then report with complete evidence packages that abuse desks can act on immediately.

We work within the five DNS abuse categories as defined by ICANN: malware distribution, botnet C2 infrastructure, phishing, pharming, and spam used to propagate the above.

All work is conducted under responsible disclosure principles. We coordinate disclosures with affected registrars, registries, CERTs, and law enforcement before any public release.
