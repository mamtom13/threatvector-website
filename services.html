DNS ABUSE INVESTIGATION

In-depth analysis of domain registration patterns, DNS record anomalies, fast-flux behavior, DGA activity, and abusive domain lifecycles across registries and registrars.

02
MALWARE C2 TRACKING

Continuous monitoring and correlation of command-and-control infrastructure. Map domain-to-IP relationships, hosting patterns, ASN data, and active C2 beacon endpoints.

03
ABUSE EVIDENCE COLLECTION

Structured collection of abuse indicators into defensible evidence packages for registrar and registry submission.

REGISTRAR REPORTING SUPPORT
